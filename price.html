Pricing

Regarding Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced:

The first lesson will be free! I want us to get to know each other a bit, understand what you’re looking for from these lessons, and to make arrangements going forwards.

Afterwards, I’ll be charging £20/hour. I accept Paypal, Lydia or simple bank transfers.





Regarding the Children's group sessions:

The first session will be free, too! I want the group to get to know each other and introduce ourselves. This session will let me see where your children stand in French.

I’ll be charging £5/30 minutes, and £2.5/15 minutes. You can either pay as we go along or 4 lessons at a time. Like above, I accept Paypal, Lydia or bank transfers.
