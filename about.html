About

Hi there! I’m Camille de Guerry, a French native from Les Sables d’Olonne. I studied English at university in France, and as soon as I completed my degree I decided to move to Kent. That’s where I am now! I learned to love the English language primarily through watching Doctor Who and Merlin. I found that simply listening to conversations was an incredibly immersive and intuitive way to learn. The accent, speech patterns, vocabulary… it’s all included in everyday conversation when using useful, everyday words.

I gave lessons in French throughout my degree, but I am not a teacher. I don’t have the grammatical knowledge in French to offer lessons to students trying to pass exams. Instead, we’d just talk and I will teach you about words and expressions French people actually use, all whilst working on fluency. We would focus on whatever aspects of your French you want to improve, whilst having a (hopefully!) fun conversation.
